The Atomic Habits Method

We don't force you to quit instantly. Instead, after each cigarette, your interval increases by 15 minutes. Small, manageable steps lead to massive long-term results.

👀

Always in Sight

With iOS Live Activities on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island, your current interval is always visible, helping you stay focused.

💡

Motivation Cards

Read personalized and default motivation cards to push through cravings and prevent relapses.

✨

Automatic Transition

Once your smoke-free interval naturally reaches 24 hours, Next15 automatically switches to "Smoke-Free" mode.
